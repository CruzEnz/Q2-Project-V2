Japan is a wonderful country that brings us many locations and destinations where you could explore and enjoy.

Here are the top 3 destinations to pick from:

Tokyo

Tokyo became one of the world's most active major cities, boasting attractions such as cutting-edge technology, information, culture, and fashion, as well as a high level of public safety.

Okinawa

Okinawa Prefecture is the only region in Japan that is in the subtropical zone, and is blessed with a warm climate all year round. An ocean with coral reefs, where schools of colorful tropical fish swim about, sustains various forms of life.

Osaka

Osaka is a charming, relaxed city best known for its food, fun and nightlife.
